We've been Making The Delicious Ghee Since 1972

Shree Thirupathi Ghee, Rich in aroma and taste. Shree Thirupathi Ghee was founded by T. Manickam Chettiyar in 1972 at Rasipuram, Tamil Nadu.

We have strong mission towards manufacturing the best quality ghee for the customers in traditional way and it known for purity, quality, hygienic.

Shree Thirupathi Ghee source natural and fresh butter from several villages in southern region and leading dairies all over India and manufacture ghee with greatest quality ever.

Benefits of Ghee

Energy Booster

Medium-chain fatty acids are another kinds of fats that make up ghee. The body definitely finds them extremely useful. The liver can process, burn, and turn these fats into energy, without contributing to gaining weight and passing into the adipose tissue. Ghee benefits people who have high-energy, active lifestyles like athletes. The clarified butter can boost energy to help through a tough, busy day.

Enhance Immunity and Digestive power

Butyric oils present in cow ghee is a kind of short- chain fatty acid. In the intestine, there is a populace of beneficial bacteria that are constantly engaged in converting fiber into butyric acid that is used by the body for energy and for supporting the intestinal wall. Consuming ghee instantly gives the required butyric acid that supplements what is produced in the body. When you take ghee, you are actually taking in the combined benefits of probiotics, prebiotics, and fiber.

Anti-inflammatory

Ayurvedic medicine has regularly used ghee to treat burns and swelling. It contains large quantities of butyrate, a fatty acid that has been linked to an immune system response that soothes inflammation. The substance also has anti-viral properties and contributes to a healthy digestive system by helping heal and repair the stomach lining. This can be particularly valuable for individuals with intestinal disorders like Crohnâ€™s disease.

Moisturize dry skin and hair

In addition to eating ghee, some people apply it topically as a creamy salve for wounds, burns, or rashes. Research has confirmed that ghee used by Indian women as a topical moisturizer to relieve dry complexions. Application on the scalp also fights dryness and encourages the growth of thick, lustrous hair.

Ease absorbing nutrients

Ghee helps in transportation and easy absorption of nutrients and medicinal compounds in the body and is therefore highly used in the Ayurveda system of medicine to prepare several herbal medicine formulations.
